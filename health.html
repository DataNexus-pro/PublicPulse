Exercise Tips

  • Start with a warm-up to get your muscles ready.
  • Incorporate both cardio and strength training into your routine.
  • Stay hydrated and drink plenty of water.
  • Listen to your body and avoid overexertion.
  • Cool down and stretch after your workout.

Diet Food

  • Include a variety of fruits and vegetables in your diet.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • Opt for lean proteins such as chicken, fish, and legumes.
  • Limit your intake of added sugars and saturated fats.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
